Most events

Jeddah Season 2022:
Jeddah Season events are held in nine different regions in Jeddah,
including: Jeddah Superdome, Jeddah Pier, Jeddah Art Promenade, Jeddah Jungle,
Prince Majid Park, Jeddah Yacht Club, City Walk, Al Balad,
and Fusion. King Abdullah Sports City (Cirque du Soleil).

Taif Rose Festival 2022:
The love story of roses ends with a magical
festival! In Taif, this story is rooted in conscience and history.
The region, due to its mild weather and mountainous terrain, annually produces
about 500 million roses, and the Taif rose has become one of the most famous
perfumes in the Arab world.

AlUla Festivals:
Every year, AlUla hosts wonderful festivals,
which it usually holds towards the end of the year,
when the region is highlighted as a museum in itself in the open air.
And Al-Ula, which has been approved as one of the global human monuments by
UNESCO, keeps pace with contemporary life with passion, and the most wonderful
festivals and parties are held in it.

Riyadh Season:
is a global entertainment festival that takes place
in the city of Riyadh, the capital of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia,
and aims to transform it into a global tourist entertainment destination,
in accordance with the objectives of the Quality of Life Program,
one of the Saudi Vision 2030 programs. Riyadh Season 2022 is the Guinness
Book of Records as the most party in which fireworks are launched from drones pilot.
